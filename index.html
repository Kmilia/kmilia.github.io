Hi! I am Camélia Guerraoui, a Double-Degree Master Student in Natural Language Processing (NLP) at Tohoku University Japan and at INSA Lyon France. My research interests are argumentation mining, automatized diagnostic feedback and human-interaction systems.

In parallel to research, I am working in the start up Rurio, which focuses on promoting the Futaba region. I am in charge of the management of the iro magazine, and of the organisation of the PaletteCamp tour.

On my free time, I enjoy acting on stage, testing a new café and watching movies.
